I made this over the course of about a day, give or take half an hour the day before. Spiritually it's a jam game, given the amount of time I put into it, but it wasn't really made for a jam.

It's a short little highscore game where you have to solve small easy tasks as they slide onto your desk at increasing speeds. Nothing all too special, but I think it's fun!

I really just made it because I had the idea of small little slips with basic maths equations sliding onto the desk, realised I could make it quite easily. Then I did.

Anyway, that's all really. Hopefully it won't be another year before I make something else new :P
